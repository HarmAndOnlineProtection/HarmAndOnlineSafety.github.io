Harm & Online Safety

Education, awareness, and reporting for harmful online activity

Purpose of This Site

Harm & Online Safety is an educational resource explaining how harmful online groups operate, why they are dangerous, and how people can protect themselves and report abuse.

This site does not promote or endorse any group.

764-Style Networks

764 is a label used for decentralized online extortion and abuse networks that rely on fear, coercion, and isolation.

These networks are disrupted through reporting, arrests, and platform enforcement.

Harm-Oriented Online Networks

Harm-focused communities normalize harassment and psychological abuse. They often collapse due to exposure, internal conflict, and moderation.

VRChat – Reported Crasher Groups

Some VRChat groups are reported as engaging in disruptive behavior such as instance crashing and coordinated harassment.

These groups lose access through reporting tools and platform enforcement.

Laughing Coffin

Laughing Coffin is described online as a COM-style group and has also been associated with reported crasher activity.

Visibility and enforcement significantly limit these groups.

Other Reported Harmful Groups

  • Use of intimidation and fear
  • Anonymity and burner accounts
  • Isolation of targets
  • Encouragement of harmful behavior

These groups frequently fragment or disappear due to exposure.
